MintMe Arbitrage Guide

Educational Web3 documentation for identifying price differences between MintMe and on-chain pools

STEP 1

Select Blockchain

Choose any token from supported chains such as BSC, Solana, Polygon, or Ethereum. Avoid random or inactive tokens.

STEP 2

Copy Contract Address

Copy the contract address from MintMe. Verify the token on-chain before doing anything.

STEP 3

Check Web3 Liquidity

Search the token on GeckoTerminal. Confirm that a real liquidity pool exists.

STEP 4

Compare Prices

Compare MintMe sell orders with Web3 pool price. If MintMe prices are higher and community is active, the opportunity may exist.

STEP 5

Buy Small Amount

Buy only $0.10 – $0.30 worth of tokens from the Web3 pool. Never go big without testing.

STEP 6

Deposit & Sell

Deposit the token into MintMe and place a sell order slightly below the highest market price.

STEP 7

Skip When Needed

If MintMe price is lower than Web3 pool or no liquidity exists, skip the token immediately.

STEP 8

Wait Patiently

Wait for buyers naturally. Arbitrage is about patience, not speed.

⚠ Important Disclaimer

Never invest without proper research. Avoid MintMe blockchain-only tokens without external liquidity. This guide is educational and does not guarantee profit.
